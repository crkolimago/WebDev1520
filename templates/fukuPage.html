About

In 2015, inspired by the success of Taiwanese "Tapioca Tea" in California and all over the world, Ting Yen set out to create Fuku Tea. Fuku Tea specializes in bubble tea with an added twist. With over 25 drinks to choose from, and up to 6 options for customization for each, the combinations are endless. From premium hot loose leaf teas to delicious specialty milk teas to snacks and toppings and smoothies , there truly is something for everybody at Fuku Tea.
